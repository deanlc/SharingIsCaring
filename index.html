The FBI has a court order demanding that Apple helps unlock an iPhone used by the gunman behind the San Bernardino terror attack in 2015, Syed Rizwan Farook. Farook and his wife killed a total of 14 people in the California city last December before the two of them were fatally shot by police.

Apple is being ordered by the FBI to create a backdoor into the iPhone's operating system, however them creating this technology would open the possibility that not only this iPhone can be accessed.

If this technology was to fall into the wrong hands - and keep in mind how highly sought after it would be - the damage casued would be irreversible.


Many tech giants have chosen to support Apple in its fight against the FBI.
